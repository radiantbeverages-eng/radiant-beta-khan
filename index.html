💾 Backup & Restore
ℹ️ About your data:
Your sales data is saved in your browser's local storage. It is not deleted when you clear browsing history or cache.

⚠️ Data is only lost if you tap "Clear Site Data" or "Clear All Data" in your browser settings.

💡 Tip: Add this app to your Home Screen (Share → Add to Home Screen on iOS, or Install App on Android) for the most reliable storage.
Export Backup
Saves all your entries, distributors, raw material stock, and settings as a .json file to your device.
Restore
Restore from Backup
Select a previously exported .json backup file to restore all data. Current data will be replaced.
Emergency Backups
Radiant automatically saves a full snapshot of your data every 6 hours — to this device and to the cloud — so you always have a recent copy to fall back on. Snapshots older than 7 days are deleted automatically to save space.
Google Drive Auto-Backup
Connect your Google account to automatically save a backup to your Google Drive every midnight. Keeps the last 30 days. Free, no extra accounts needed.
